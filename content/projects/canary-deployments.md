---
title: "Canary Deployments on Kubernetes"
date: 2026-01-01
---

### Context
Production Kubernetes platform serving live user traffic.

### Problem
Deployments were risky and rollbacks were manual, increasing the chance of production incidents.

### Solution
- GitOps-based deployment workflow
- Canary releases with controlled traffic shifting
- Metrics-driven automated rollback

### Impact
- Reduced deployment-related incidents
- Faster and safer releases
- Improved confidence during production changes
